Camp Nou

By Vinay D Bharadwaj

Camp Nou(Catalan) meaning new field, often referred to in English as the Nou Camp is a football stadium in Barcelona, Spain. It opened in 1957 and has been the home stadium of FC Barcelona since its completion. With a seating capacity of 99,354, it is the largest stadium in Spain and Europe, and the fourth largest football stadium in the world in capacity.
